I waited… and waited.

I thought you'd see me one day — the way I saw you every day.
But time kept moving, and you never looked back.

Every message you didn’t send… every moment you didn’t notice — I noticed.
I kept believing you’d turn around.
I was always there, quietly holding on while pretending I wasn’t hurting.

But the waiting started to feel endless. The silence, suffocating.
I waited for a call that never came, a gesture that never happened.

And I began to wonder — does love require silence? Or does it demand to be heard?

I waited for you to love me back in the way I needed.
But every time I waited, my heart broke a little more.

And now, I’m just exhausted. I still love you, but I can’t keep waiting.
