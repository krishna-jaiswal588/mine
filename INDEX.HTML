I’ve always loved you.

Even when I said nothing, my heart was full of words for you.
I stayed. I cared. I never stopped loving you.

You were my first thought every morning, and my last every night.
I didn’t ask for anything… just for you to feel what I couldn’t say.
My love was silent, but it was screaming inside me.

All these years, I’ve waited for the moment you’d see the truth in my eyes.
The truth that I never walked away, never gave up — I stayed because I loved you.

But you never looked back. And still, I remained.

I waited through every storm, every silence — believing that one day, you’d hear my heart.
But you never heard it. Instead, you walked away.

And now, I’m left with only these words — words I can’t say to you anymore.
