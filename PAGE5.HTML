Still holding on.

After everything, I’m still here — because my heart never learned to stop loving you.
I’ve cried, I’ve broken, I’ve questioned everything.

But even in my weakest moments, my heart still whispers your name.
You were never just someone to me — you were everything.

I don’t know what the future holds, but I know this: I’ve loved you with all of me.
And no matter how tired I get, that love won’t just disappear.

I’m not letting go. Not yet. Maybe not ever.
I’ll keep holding on — even if it’s in silence.
