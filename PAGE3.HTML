I was invisible to you.

I stood right in front of you, but you never saw me.
I spoke my heart, but you didn’t hear it.

You were too busy with your life, to see mine crumbling.
I was always there, waiting… but you never looked.

The hardest part was pretending everything was fine when inside, I was breaking.
You were my everything, but I was nothing to you.

I waited and waited, hoping one day you’d notice.
But I was invisible. A shadow in the background of your life.
